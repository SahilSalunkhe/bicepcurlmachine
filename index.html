BENCH

बेंच

The Bench is a foundational, highly versatile compound setup used primarily for targeting and developing the chest, shoulders, and triceps. By adjusting its angle—whether flat, incline, or decline—it allows you to shift the mechanical focus to different areas of the upper body. It serves as the core platform for essential strength-building movements like the dumbbell press, dumbbell fly, and classic barbell bench press.
